My Experience :Google India Challenge Scholarship 2018

May 13, 2018

2018   ·   formatting   math     ·   sample-posts  

It was late December I guess I was scrolling down on my Twitter feed and I found a tweet about the Google Scholarship program. As I was very interested in Mobile App Development and Programming since my school days and it was a wonderful opportunity to learn something new and start the journey as a Developer. I did not think much and fill the form immediately!

I was very excited!

7th Feb,2018

I almost forgot about the scholarship program that I had applied for due to my exams and vacations!

I got surprised!

It was one of the best days of my life I was so happy and excited.

14th Feb, 2018

The scholarship was officially announced and started. We joined the forum and slack. We introduced ourselves in the #introductions channel in slack and forum. It was really amazing I met so many talented scholars all over the country.

At the beginning of the course, I was facing difficulty just like many of us but subsequently, with the help of mentors, forum and #live_help channel in the slack community all of my queries are solved. There were channels for each lesson, state channels for each state,live_help channels, etc. My favorite channels were #random,#live_help,#external_resources and #AssamThere are also weekly AMA(ask me anything!) sessions and LIVE help sessions conducted by our Community Managers and Tech Guru, Akshit Jain sir and Iip Permana. Those sessions used to be very helpful and awesome. After few weeks quizzes and collaborative projects started and it was an amazing learning experience for me with the community

About The Course

The course was is excellently covered a lot of concepts of Android Developments as I was a beginner then I loved the course! The Course was by Udacity and Google developers itself, the duration of the challenge course was 90 days for PHASE-I. The best part of this Course is the Awesome Community ❤️.I would like to thank Akshit jain sir(Student Experience Lead) for guiding and inspiring us from the beginning and all the mentors Harshit Dwivedi ,Abhishek Raj,Nisha Shinde and all the Fellow Scholar for being part of my amazing journey.

After the Phase 1 of the Scholarship Program, I was selected for the full Nanodegree course sponsored by Google.

It has been a great experience for me being a Google-Udacity Scholar.I’m very happy that I have learned a lot of stuff about ANDROID AND JAVA.

Thank You Google and Udacity ❤️

Thanks for reading!! P.S. This is my first blog on Internet!!
